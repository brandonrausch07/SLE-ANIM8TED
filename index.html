What are MONSTRS?

MONSTRS are a collection of 10,000 unique one-of-a-kind collectibles that bridge the physical and digital worlds. Each MONSTR comes paired with a premium physical trading card that unlvoks augmented reality (AR) content, bringing your MONSTR to life through your phone. By combining cutting edge technology with high-quality collectibles, MONSTRS offers and innovative and interactive experience for fans and collectors.

When you purchase a MONSTR, you can register on MONSTRS.com to claim your physical card. The registration process verifies ownership and collects your shipping details, ensuring the card is mailed to you securely. Each card is linked to its corresponding NFT via blockchain technology, providing proof of authenticity and enabling seamlass rescale. If you sell your MONSTR NFT, the card's AR features will transfer to the new owner upon their registration, ensuing the experience remains exclusive to the current NFT holder. With additional benefits like exclusive content, early acess to future drops, and collectible rarity ties, MONSTRS is designed to captivate collectors and create a thriving community around these groundbreaking hybrid collectibles. Whether you're here for the stunning artwork, the immersive AR features, or the opportunity to own a truly unique piece of MONSTRS universe, you're part of an exciting new chapter in the world of collectibles.
